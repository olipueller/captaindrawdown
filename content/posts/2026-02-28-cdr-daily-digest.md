The [results](https://www.newscientist.com/article/2517171-ocean-geoengineering-trial-finds-no-evidence-of-harm-to-marine-life/), announced at the Ocean Sciences Meeting in Glasgow:

- **2â€“10 tonnes of COâ‚‚ removed** in the first four days; up to 50 tonnes estimated total
